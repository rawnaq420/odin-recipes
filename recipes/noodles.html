Noodles

Description

Noodles are long, thin strips of cooked dough. They're made mostly from wheat and water. People eat them in many ways - in soups, fried dishes, or with tasty sauces. Noodles are a common food in many countries and give you energy when you eat them.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup water
  • Structured wheat flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon green coriander leaves
  • Eggs

Directions

  1. Boil water
  2. Add some pinch of salt if desired
  3. Add egg noodles in the boiling water. Stir gently to prevent sticking each other
  4. For dried egg noodles, cook them for about 5-7 minutes
  5. For normal case, cook them only for about 2-3 minutes
  6. Serve then
